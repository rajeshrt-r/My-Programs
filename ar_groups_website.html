Frequently Asked Questions

🏠 Construction Services

Our Standard package starts at ₹1,900/sq.ft and our Premium package at ₹2,400/sq.ft. These rates include all basic construction work, materials, and labour. Custom luxury packages are also available based on your specific requirements. Use our interactive cost calculator above for a detailed estimate.

Yes, we handle everything from architectural design and government permits to material procurement, construction, interior finishing, and final handover. You get a dedicated project manager with weekly WhatsApp progress updates including photos and videos.

All our constructions come with a 10-year structural warranty covering the foundation, columns, beams, and slabs. Additionally, we provide a 1-year finishing warranty covering plumbing, electrical, and paint work.

We serve Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, Salem, Erode, and all surrounding districts. For locations outside these areas, please contact us to discuss availability.

🏍️ Vehicle Finance

You'll need: Aadhar card, PAN card, 3 months bank statement, salary slips (for salaried) or ITR (for self-employed), and two passport-size photos. We help you through the entire documentation process.

Most loans are approved within 24-48 hours with proper documentation. Our in-house finance team works with multiple lending partners to get you the best rate and fastest approval.

Absolutely. Every bike goes through our comprehensive 150-point inspection covering engine condition, electricals, body and frame, tyres and brakes, and complete document verification. We provide a detailed inspection report with every purchase.

The standard minimum down payment is 20% of the bike value. However, for select models and customers with strong credit profiles, lower down payment options may be available. Use our EMI calculator above to plan your purchase.
